My Gallery Page

I spent 3 weeks in London this past summer, and I saw so much culture and history... so many gorgeous things that I wanted to hold onto forever. The historic architecture contrasts breathtakingly with the modern bohemoth skyscrapers in a dramatic and stunning way.

I probably walked about 20 miles a day, looking up and around at everything. The rose gardens are magic, the sculptural qualities of all of the architecture... If I had been using a film camera I would have gone completely broke. Everything about the parks and green spaces felt so clean and natural. Here are some shots I took.
